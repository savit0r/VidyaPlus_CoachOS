# Milestone 1.0 Audit Report: Core Platform

## Executive Summary

Milestone 1.0 has successfully achieved its "Definition of Done". The platform now supports multi-tenant operations for coaching institutes, covering the entire lifecycle from enrollment to fee collection and delegation.

The recent audit specifically focused on the **Delegation Engine** and **Staff Portal synchronization**, ensuring that granular permissions delegated by owners are strictly enforced in both the UI and the API.

## Integration & Security Audit

### 1. Cross-Phase Wiring
- **Enrollment → Fees**: Enrolling a student successfully triggers admission and monthly fee generation.
- **Attendance → Notifications**: Marking a student absent auto-generates owner alerts.
- **Staff → RBAC**: Staff logins correctly scoped via `auth.middleware.ts` and `DashboardDrillDown.tsx`.

### 2. Teacher Data Isolation (Hardened)
- Teachers can only see batches they are assigned to.
- Teachers can only see student lists for their specific batches.
- Teachers can only mark/view attendance for their assigned batches.

## Verification Results

- **Unit Tests**: All core logic in `auth.store.ts` and shared permissions verified.
- **API Tests**: Manual verification of 403 Forbidden responses when staff attempt unauthorized access.
- **UI UX**: High-fidelity Mintlify design applied across all 12 feature modules.

## Next Steps

With Milestone 1.0 completed and audited, the platform is ready for pilot deployment.
